What even is this website?

This site started in the last decade as a blog after Luke graduated college. Then life happened, the site went down, and Luke got too busy to do anything but hold onto the domain.

Years later, Luke needed to get the rust off his web dev skills. HTML! CSS! Github Actions! An AWS S3 bucket! These are the ingredients necessary to create... a simple website to host a tech blog, a self-promotion site, and some goofy fonts and colors while experimenting with 'minimal' website design.

This site might evolve into something more than a tech blog. Maybe it'll be a hub for different projects, services, or businesses Luke is or will be involved with. Maybe it'll just be an over-complicated resume. Who knows. But for now, just think of it as Luke's static website.

In the meantime, check the links up top and have a wonderful day!
